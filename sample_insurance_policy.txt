NATIONAL PARIVAR MEDICLAIM PLUS POLICY (NPMPP)

POLICY TERMS AND CONDITIONS

1. GRACE PERIOD FOR PREMIUM PAYMENT
The grace period for premium payment under this policy is 15 days from the due date. If the premium is not paid within the grace period, the policy will lapse.

2. WAITING PERIOD FOR PRE-EXISTING DISEASES (PED)
There is a waiting period of 48 months (4 years) for pre-existing diseases to be covered under this policy. Any medical condition that existed before the policy start date will not be covered during this waiting period.

3. MATERNITY EXPENSES COVERAGE
This policy does NOT cover maternity expenses. All pregnancy-related costs, including delivery, prenatal care, and postnatal care are excluded from coverage.

4. WAITING PERIOD FOR CATARACT SURGERY
There is a waiting period of 24 months (2 years) for cataract surgery under this policy. The surgery must be performed after the waiting period to be eligible for coverage.

5. ORGAN DONOR MEDICAL EXPENSES
Medical expenses for organ donors are covered under this policy, subject to the same terms and conditions as other medical procedures. The donor must be a family member as defined in the policy.

6. NO CLAIM DISCOUNT (NCD)
The No Claim Discount (NCD) offered in this policy is as follows:
- 5% discount for 1 claim-free year
- 10% discount for 2 claim-free years
- 15% discount for 3 claim-free years
- 20% discount for 4 claim-free years
- 25% discount for 5 or more claim-free years

7. PREVENTIVE HEALTH CHECK-UP BENEFIT
Yes, there is a benefit for preventive health check-ups. The policy provides coverage up to Rs. 5,000 per year for preventive health check-ups, subject to the overall policy limits.

8. HOSPITAL DEFINITION
A 'Hospital' is defined as any institution that:
- Is registered with the local health authorities
- Has qualified medical practitioners
- Has facilities for diagnosis and treatment of illness or injury
- Provides 24-hour nursing services
- Has at least 10 in-patient beds

9. AYUSH TREATMENTS COVERAGE
AYUSH treatments (Ayurveda, Yoga, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy) are covered under this policy up to 25% of the sum insured, subject to a maximum of Rs. 50,000 per year.

10. SUB-LIMITS FOR PLAN A
For Plan A, there are the following sub-limits:
- Room rent: Maximum Rs. 5,000 per day
- ICU charges: Maximum Rs. 10,000 per day
- These limits are within the overall sum insured of the policy.

POLICY EXCLUSIONS:
- Cosmetic surgery
- Dental treatment (except due to accident)
- Pre-existing diseases during waiting period
- Maternity expenses
- Self-inflicted injuries
- War-related injuries
- Nuclear radiation injuries

This policy is valid for one year from the date of commencement and can be renewed annually.
